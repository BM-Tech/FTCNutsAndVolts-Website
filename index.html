Who are we?

We are a First Tech Challenge(FTC) Robotics team.

What is FTC Robotics?

FTC is First Tech Challenge. You can read more about them here.

What makes our team special?

We are a self-driven group of passionate students whose ambition is to build robots while supporting the community.

We do more than
build robots.

Apart from building robots, we have strived greatly to reach out to and help the community. One way we have done this is through Chroniva, an NPO one of our members founded. Chroniva is an organization that specializes in teaching students about advanced STEM concepts. We collaborate with them on videos and workshops, teaching scholars on robotics-related concepts like Cadding. Moreover, we have linked with another FTC Robotics team: Future #14473. We try to help and share materials and opportunities with each other. We are also partnered with STEM Outside the Box, another organization that endeavors to serve underprivileged kids by teaching STEM. Overall, with these partnerships and links, we want to spread our valuable Robotics and STEM experiences to others in our society.
